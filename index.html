One More Treasure Chest Privacy Policy

We do not keep or sell any personal information. We only collect what is necessary for a smooth gameplay experience.

Data Deletion

Users can delete their account and all associated games data collected by Google through their Play Games profile or Google MyAccount.
If Google collects Play Games Services data for an individual game, users can delete the data through their Play Games profile.

Google Play Games Services Plugin for Unity

We use Google Play Games Services API to allow users for sign in, achievements, and cloud saves read/write.

    The Play Games Services SDKs collect the following data automatically:
  • Game Service: Collects the following Games account data when a user creates and updates their games account:
    1. Gamer Identity (Gamertag, avatar)
  • Analytics: Collects analytics data to improve the stability of our SDKs and make product improvements.
  • Diagnostics: Collects diagnostics data to improve the stability of our SDKs and make product improvements.
